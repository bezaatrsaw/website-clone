Short Bio

Mamusha Fenta is a Bible expositor and conference speaker residing in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. He came to a personal relationship with Christ at the age of 16 through the testimony of a friend and a miraculous conversion experience through the Holy Spirit. His involvement in the Christian students’ fellowship gave him his first exposure to ministry during his college years. He soon responded to a calling to full-time ministry and has been serving with different capacities since 1992.

He has been a regular speaker and teacher in the fast growing University Fellowships of Ethiopia and speaker and teacher in many assemblies and churches in Ethiopia and throughout the world.

He currently leads a vibrant ministry that supports and promotes Christian leadership development, church planting into all areas of the country, and mission efforts from Ethiopia to the region.

Received his BTH degree from Evangelical Theological College in Addis Ababa, his MTH from University of Wales through Mattersey Hall in the UK and completed a DMIN at Gordon-Conwell Theological Seminary in the USA.

Prior to his current role, Dr Mamusha Fenta gave leadership to the Pentecostal Theological College in Addis Ababa for 15 years. Mamusha and his wife Leke are parents of four beautiful children.
